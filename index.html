About GOOD SPIRITS

Good Spirits is an experiment in creating community around tasty drinks and local ingredients. Brought to you by Key City Spirits and Barbox Mobile Bar, Good Spirits pop-up cocktail classes pair the flavors of local distilleries and farmers with the expertise of local mixologists to bring you a curated craft cocktail experience. Get ready to roll up your sleeves, have fun, and take home some skills to share with your friends and family!

Learn to make three craft cocktails

Each class will teach you the basics of bar setup and terminology. The instructor will walk you through three unique cocktail recipes tailored for the class using all local spirits and ingredients.
